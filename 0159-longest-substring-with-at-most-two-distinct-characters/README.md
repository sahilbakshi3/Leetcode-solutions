<h2><a href="https://leetcode.com/problems/longest-substring-with-at-most-two-distinct-characters/">159. Longest Substring with At Most Two Distinct Characters</a></h2><h3>Medium</h3><hr><p>Given a string <code>s</code>, return <em>the length of the longest substring that contains at most two distinct characters</em>.</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong class="example">Example 1:</strong></p>

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong> s = "eceba"
<strong>Output:</strong> 3
<strong>Explanation:</strong>
The longest substring is "ece".
</pre>

<p><strong class="example">Example 2:</strong></p>

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong> s = "ccaabbb"
<strong>Output:</strong> 5
<strong>Explanation:</strong>
The longest substring is "aabbb".
</pre>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Constraints:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li><code>1 &lt;= s.length &lt;= 10^5</code></li>
	<li><code>s</code> consists of English letters.</li>
</ul>
